KUMARAKOM BIRD SANCTURARY

Set along the tranquil Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary is a paradise of lush greenery, serene backwaters, and exotic migratory birds. A perfect retreat for nature lovers!

Overview

A Paradise for Nature Lovers Nestled along the serene Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary is a breathtaking haven for bird watchers and nature enthusiasts. Lush green landscapes, tranquil backwaters, and the melodious calls of exotic migratory birds create a magical escape. Witness vibrant flocks of herons, egrets, kingfishers, and even rare Siberian cranes in their natural habitat.
